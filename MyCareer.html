Actuarial - Capital

Why the move to capital?

I moved to capital as I wanted to gain a more commerical understanding of how an insurance busines operates as a whole, not just the intricacies of a single product

Key Commercial skills Gained?

I gained a solid understanding of monte carlo simulation aka stochastic modeling, gaining exposure to all areas of risk modelling within an insurance business: Loss modelling, market/ investment risk, operational risk and cat risk.
I contributed to the paramterisaiton and forecasting involved in each risk and the copula and correlation matricies implemented to combined them altogether - a strong background in risk management and the awareness of correlations is key across all commercial businesses.
I learned the details of variables effecting regulatory capital requirement and its relationship to share price, rare knowledge.
I got to understand the running of an insurance business at the portfolio level, how the risk reward characterisitcs of indiviudal products pool together and the levers management can utilise to enhance ROE such as product diversification benefits and reinsurance structures.
I also pushed to build out the businesses market risk analysis capabilities, creating quarterly FX, Interest Rate and Bond return exhibits: including fan chart forecasts and probabilistic expecations.
I utilised the bond return projections to create portfolio performance projections and carry out asset allocation and efficient frontier analyis, presenting to management ideas to improve bottom line performance.
